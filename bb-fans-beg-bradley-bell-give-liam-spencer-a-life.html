Give Liam Spencer A Life!

 Posted on March 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  446 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) fans are begging head writer Bradley Bell to give Liam Spencer (Scott Clifton) a life.

They want him to stay out of Steffy Forrester’s (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) marriage to Dr. John “Finn” Finnegan (Tanner Novlan). Keep on reading to learn more.

B&B Fans Declare: Give Liam A Life!

The user 24Kasie76 posted on the SoapCentral.com Message Boards: “BELL: Can you please let Liam get a life and leave #SINN off, thank you.”

The Bold and the Beautiful fans want Liam to have his own life outside of Steffy and Finn’s marriage.

They think he’s overstepped Steffy’s boundaries to interfere with their relationship.

He already did that when he kissed Steffy more than once. He also told her that he still loves her and would’ve chosen her if he could go back in time.

B&B fans think Liam needs to get a life outside of Steffy’s affairs. They’re getting desperate and hope that Bell reads the threads over at SoapCentral.com Message Boards.

“I really REALLY hope to see Liam if BELL writes like I think he will, become interested in someone who is not madly IN love with a great loyal fierce faithful man, a true fighter for his family FINN, because it is clear, IMHO Finn and his wife soulmate Steffi Finnegan, are endgame,” the original poster declared.

This caused a heavy debate on the thread. Some B&B fans argued that not everyone loves Finn and Steffy together.

They think that Finn is a boring character who doesn’t have a back story aside from his family history.

Others are tired of seeing #SINN on their screen and think they’re a boring couple with zero chemistry.

The Bold And Beautiful Updates – Who Does Steffy Belong With?

The Bold and the Beautiful fans debated over who Steffy belongs with. Some think she’s become too boring now that she’s married to Finn.

“Not everyone loves Finn and Steffy like you do, so you can’t speak as thought you speak for everyone. Let Mr. Bell do what he does, with his show,” one fan argued.

“Speak for yourself… We’ve had like 5 yrs.+ I think of Lope which had zero chemistry. Their time is up. There are a lot of fans who would like Steam back. Lots of chemistry, good looks & love!” another wrote.

Most B&B fans are willing to give Steam another try. They would love to see Steffy happy with someone like Liam since her marriage to Finn failed.

What are your thoughts? Do you think Liam should get a life? Sound off below in the comment section.
