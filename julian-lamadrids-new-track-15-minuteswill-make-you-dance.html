Julian Lamadrid's new track '15 Minutes'will make you dance

 Posted on February 4, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  207 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Julian Lamadrid is back with a brand new track ’15 Minutes’ a hypnotising track that will not fail to have you dancing.

Born to Mexican immigrants, Julian moved to New York City from Dubai at the age of 18, crediting films such as ‘Taxi Driver’ and ‘Midnight Cowboy’ as reasons to enrol in New York University’s film school. He then graduated early upon signing with Arista Records/Sony Music Entertainment.  

Frustrated by the societal pressure to create data driven and social media-focused art, Julian set out to create an audible pathway towards the future of pop music. 

 In 2019, he released his debut album, Mala Noche, which featured the tracks, “Mess”, “Neon”, “Die Young”, “Red Car” and “Moment.” Not only did he compose and produce the entire project, Julian also directed the accompanying visuals.

Last year he released  he released “Patience” and “My Time,” both of which were entirely written and produced by Julian.

Julian has also directed and edited the video for his latest track ’15 Minutes.’ ’15 Minutes’ is him taking action and not just complaining about the state of modern pop music.

There is more music to come from Julian in the spring before the release of a full project later this year.
