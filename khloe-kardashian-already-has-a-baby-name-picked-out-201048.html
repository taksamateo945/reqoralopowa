Khloe Kardashian Already Has a Baby Name Picked Out

 Posted on January 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  233 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Khloe Kardashian isn't pregnant – yet!- but she and husband Lamar Odom already have a baby name picked out.

"Boy, it would be Luke Joseph. The girl is on her," Odom, 30, told UsMagazine.com at the Power Balance Goes All-In For A Cure event at Drai's Hollywood Tuesday.
