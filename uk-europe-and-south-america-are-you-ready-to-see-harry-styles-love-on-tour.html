UK, Europe and South America are you ready to see Harry Styles' Love On Tour?

 Posted on January 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  360 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

We knew once Harry Styles finished his super successful North American leg of his Love On Tour, that it would only be a matter of time (and a fair bit of work behind the scenes) before he announced more dates. And now that moment is here.

He has announced that he is bringing Love On Tour to the UK, Europe and South America later this year.

Not only that Harry will be doing his first-ever stadium dates. The tour will see the Adore You singer joined by special guests including Mitski on the UK dates, Arlo Parks in Dublin, Wolf Alice on select European dates and Koffee across all Latin American dates.

 The colossal 32-city outing with three brand new stadium dates in the U.K. in June, and a further four stadiums in Europe including a brand new show at Glasgow Ibrox Stadium, Manchester Emirates Old Trafford and London Wembley Stadium through June.  The tour will continue in July with rescheduled arena dates in Europe, as well as a new show at Accor Arena in Paris, before concluding in Latin America later this year. All existing tickets for rescheduled shows will remain valid.

The tour will be following all local and public health guidelines as well as venue protocols set forth at the time of each show. Fans are encouraged to visit their local venue’s website ahead of each show for the latest health & safety details and requirements for their date.

Ticket holders for the 2020 UK Arena tour dates *will be refunded* and receive an exclusive access presale to ensure priority access to the new performance at Glasgow Ibrox Stadium, Manchester Emirates Old Trafford and London Wembley Stadium. Ticket holders will be contacted by their ticket agent with details.

 Tickets go on sale for Harry Styles’ Love On Tour 2022 9am on Friday 28 January at LiveNation.co.uk
