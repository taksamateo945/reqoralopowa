NeNe Leakes Slams Rumors That She And Gregg Leakes Are Over And Also Says Shell Never Be Friends

 Posted on March 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  267 words  |  Larita Shotwell

After seeing yesterday’s RHOA episode, more fans freaked out at the thought that NeNe Leakes and Gregg Leakes are separating.

NeNe made sure to slam the rumors on Twitter, and she told everyone that what they watched was just an episode filmed back in 2018.

She also made sure to say that things are good between her and her hubby.

And more than that, when a commenter said that they were praying that she and Cynthia Bailey work things out, NeNe made it also very clear that this will never happen and they will only be co-workers.
