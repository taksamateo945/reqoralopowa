It’s apparent from the photographs that Gabrielle and Dwayne extended a heartfelt welcome to Hudson as he joined them in the celebration.

The responsibilities and obligations that we have as parents are extremely important to us. So, whether our child comes home with a question, an issue, or anything else, it is our responsibility as parents to listen to them and provide them with the greatest knowledge and feedback that we are able to provide. That doesn’t change because sexuality is involved in the situation at hand. Once Zaya, our 12-year-old daughter, returned home… and said, ‘Hey, I’d like to speak with you guys because I believe that going forward, I’m ready to live my truth.’ I would like to be referred to as she and her.
