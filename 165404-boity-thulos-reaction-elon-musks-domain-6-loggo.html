Socialite Boity Thulo was hit by an epiphany when she realised that Elon Musk was not bluffing with the new Twitter logo.

Elon Musk buys and changes the Twitter app

Tweeps have noted that their Twitter experience has changed since South African-born billionaire Elon Musk bought the messaging app.

One of the most notable changes was changing the blue bird logo to the new metallic X logo.

Twitter troll body shames Sho Madjozi

In another Twitter story on Briefly News, Xitsonga rapper and dancer Sho Madjozi became a victim of body shaming.

The international star released a video of her working on the visuals of her new single Balotelli when a troll took a jab at her slender frame, asking if she was sick.

Thankfully, other tweeps restrained the strong-willed bully until she backed off.
