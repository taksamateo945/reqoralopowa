Does Tom Brady own the Raiders?

In June 2023, Tom Brady announced that he had reached an agreement to buy a minority ownership stake in the Las Vegas Raiders.

Brady is not the only NFL player to become an owner.

He joins Peyton Manning, who owns a 10% stake in the Denver Broncos, and Aaron Rodgers, who owns a 1% stake in the Milwaukee Bucks.
